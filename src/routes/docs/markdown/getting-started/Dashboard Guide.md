# Dashboard Guide

The dashboard is your home base throughout Alchemize. From here, you can manage projects, track currencies, browse rewards, and convert currencies into Potion Mix.

---

## Information

The bar at the top of the page displays your account information including:

* Username
* Email address
* Profile picture

---

## Dashboard Overview

### Connection Status

This section shows:

* Whether your Hackatime account is connected
* Your YSWS eligibility status
* Any account-related information that may require your attention

If your Hackatime account is not connected, some features may not be available until the connection is completed.

### Recent Mixes

A **Mix** is an Alchemize project.

This section displays your most recently submitted projects, allowing you to quickly revisit your work and track your progress.

To see every project you've created, click **View All Mixes**.

---

## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions panel provides shortcuts to the most commonly used parts of Alchemize.

## Projects

The Projects page is where you can:

* View all your submitted projects
* Create new projects
* Edit existing submissions
* Track project progress

### Creating a Project

To submit a project:

1. Open the **Projects** page.
2. Click **Add Mix**.
3. Enter a project name.
4. Write a short project description. You may include features, goals, or technical details.
5. Add the GitHub repository link.
6. Add a working demo link, if available.
7. Upload a cover image or screenshot.
8. Select the project type (Software, Hardware, etc.).
9. Choose one of the current season's themes.
10. Select the corresponding Hackatime project used to track your work.

After submission, your project will be reviewed and processed according to the event rules.

## Currency System

Every theme in Alchemize has its own currency.

As you spend time building projects and logging hours through Hackatime, you earn currency associated with the theme your project belongs to.

For Season 1:

| Theme         | Currency   |
| ------------- | ---------- |
| Indie Gamedev | Aqua Regia |
| Endless       | Redstone   |
| No Internet   | Glowstone  |

The more you build, the more currency you earn.

Currencies can be:

* Spent directly in the shop
* Combined with other currencies to create Potion Mix

Potion Mix can then be used to unlock additional rewards.

---

## Shop

The Shop is where you spend the currencies you've earned.

Different rewards require different currencies depending on their category.

### Aqua Regia Rewards

Rewards related to the **Indie Gamedev** theme.

These can be purchased using **Aqua Regia**.

### Redstone Rewards

Rewards related to the **Endless** theme.

These can be purchased using **Redstone**.

### Glowstone Rewards

Rewards related to the **No Internet** theme.

These can be purchased using **Glowstone**.

### Potion Mix Rewards

Some rewards are not tied to a specific theme.

These special rewards can be purchased using **Potion Mix**, which is created by combining thematic currencies.

The shop inventory may change between seasons, so check back regularly for new rewards.

---

## Trade

The Trade Portal allows you to combine thematic currencies and transform them into **Potion Mix**.

### How It Works

1. Open the **Trade** page.
2. Enter the amount of Aqua Regia, Redstone, and/or Glowstone you want to use.
3. Experiment with different combinations.
4. Review the Potion Mix output.
5. Click **Convert** to complete the trade.

Using currencies from multiple themes often results in better Potion Mix conversions, encouraging participants to explore more than one theme.

Once created, Potion Mix can be spent on eligible rewards in the shop.

---

## Refer

Every participant receives a unique referral link.

You can share this link with friends who may be interested in joining Alchemize.

### How Referrals Work

1. Open the **Refer** page.
2. Copy your unique referral link.
3. Share it with friends.
4. When someone joins using your link, the referral is recorded automatically.

### Referral Rewards

Participants who successfully refer others may receive:

* Bonus currency
* Exclusive seasonal rewards
* Special shop items
* Referral leaderboard recognition

Specific referral rewards may vary between seasons and will be announced alongside each season's reward pool.
