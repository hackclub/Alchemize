# HCA Configs
Hack Club Auth aka HCA is a platform that whole of Hack Club uses to make its authentication infrastrucutre. hosted on auth.hackclub.com it is a crucial part of Hack Club infra. There you can configure a bunch of things about yourself, somethings like birthdate, names and address are required for you to ship your project

--- 

## What does HCA config here means?
It basically means if our database(encrypted) has the required details about you to ship your project, this includes
- Address
- Birthdate
- First and Last names

--- 

## Why is my HCA Misconfigured?

If you are a new person, the HCA configs may show something like
"Address Misconfigured"

Don't worry, this is nothing to worry about. Just go to [auth.hackclub.com](auth.hackclub.com) and then you can add your details there


### For Birthdates and Names
Go to [auth.hackclub.com](auth.hackclub.com) and then My Info and add your info there


### For Address
[auth.hackclub.com](auth.hackclub.com) and then Mailing Addresses and add your address there

After it, there should be a red button called "Re-login" just where it shows HCA configs, click on there. alterantively you can click here [Re-login](/auth) and it should be fixed and green!
