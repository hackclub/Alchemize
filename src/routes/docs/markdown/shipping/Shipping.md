## Shipping your projects
If you have doubts like - How to ship my project? What are the requirements to ship a project? What types of ships get rejected?...
The answers to all your questions are here!

## What is shipping
Shipping is the process of making your project shareable.
A project creates value only when others can access it. Shipping turns your work from a private experiment into something that can be used, tested, and improved. It's the step that transforms an idea sitting on your computer into something that can make a real impact.

## The 3 minimums

### Document your project
Describe it in your own words. Write a short description in simple words

What inspired you to build the project?

Attach some photos, videos and screenshots of your project.

### Make it Organized
All the files should be easily accessable and well organized. Keeping it organized makes it easy for someone to go through youe project and see what you have done.
